New Zealand Tourism Research Institute (NZTRI) is a company which is all about travel and tourism. the New Zealand Tourism Research Institute is an organisation that focuses on bringing ‘timely and innovative research solutions for the tourism industry’. Their main goal is to develop a sustainable and profitable tourism industry that gives citizens, organisations and visitors tangible benefits.

This website was created to give the opportunity to allow less media exposed communities to promote their local destination through authentic community-generated content comprising of story-telling, photographs, videos and sounds produced from community members through an online platform.
